📊 Elite_Brain-Spine-Hospital

An **interactive healthcare analytics dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to monitor hospital performance and patient insights. This dashboard helps analyze important hospital metrics through **clear visuals, dynamic charts, and filters**. 📊

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Elite Brain & Spine Hospital Dashboard** provides a data-driven view of hospital operations. It allows users to quickly understand patient flow, waiting time, satisfaction levels, and demographic insights.

With interactive elements and visually appealing charts, the dashboard makes healthcare data **easy to understand and analyze**.

---

## ✨ Key Features

📊 **Total Patients Analysis** – Track the total number of patients visiting the hospital.  
⏱️ **Average Wait Time** – Monitor patient waiting time to improve hospital efficiency.  
⭐ **Patient Satisfaction Score** – Measure patient experience and service quality.  
🏥 **Admission Status** – Compare admitted vs non-admitted patients.  
👥 **Gender Distribution** – Visual breakdown of male and female patients.  
📈 **Age Group Insights** – Analyze patients by different age categories.  
📅 **Monthly Filter** – Dynamic slicer to explore data month-wise.

---

- 🎛 Interactive Filters (Slicers)
- 📉 Data Analysis Techniques
